Matador’s mission is to accelerate  all people’s prosperity.

At Matador, we want to help you build relationships around finance with a community you trust, so you can be more effective and knowledgeable in what you’re doing.

For too long, investing has happened in isolation – whether you’re doing it online or through a financial advisor, the experience is overall pretty lonely. We’re starting with the loneliest place in wealth creation and management: the stock market.

We operate through our shared values and commitments.

Focus on our mission.

As a mission-driven company, we are inspired and motivated to accelerate people’s prosperity.

Empower our community and ourselves.

Whether it's making Matador’s products & services better, or helping people advance their investing strategies, we commit to focusing our energy on what’s most empowering for our community.

Make it better.

We commit to taking stock of the status quo with curiosity and acumen, and to then bias for action when things need to be improved.

Be transparent.

We commit to being as transparent as we can with ourselves, our community, and the industry.
