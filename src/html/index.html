Stocks, made friendly.

Matador gives you the power of community, so you’re more empowered when you invest in the stock market. It’s friendly, easy, and free.

Friendly: #SquadGoals

Things suck in isolation. That’s why Matador is bringing the power of community to investing. Discuss, learn, and invest with people you trust.

Easy: #Beliefs

Matador makes it easy to learn, follow, and invest in the things you believe are worth it. From cannabis and tech to tried-and-true industries, Matador has you covered with curated investing themes that make it easy to get going.

Free: #YepFree

Unlike other apps, Matador is really-for-real free. We’re not skimming off your portfolio, and we don’t charge you for trading. We’re working on a community-powered revenue stream, but we’ll be super transparent when that’s in place.
