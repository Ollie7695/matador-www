A simple way to grow your money

Matador is a simple place to grow your money. From one account, earn a baseline 2.5% interest on your cash, or invest your money in the stock market with the confidence and joy of the Matador community by your side.

Friendly: #SquadGoals

The stock market shouldn't be scary or complex. That's why Matador is bringing the power of community to investing – so we can all explore, share, and discover companies and industries to invest in.

Simple: #Growth

We believe you should be able to grow your money in one great place, instead of trying to keep track of a bunch of apps. From interest to investing, Matador gives you a simple way to grow your money.

Free: #YepFree

Matador is free to use and we collect $0 in commission fees, regardless of the amount you are investing. There are no account minimums, so invest as much or as little as you are comfortable with. See below for details.
